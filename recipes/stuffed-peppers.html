Stuffed Peppers

Want to make your own tasty peppers? Here's the guide for you!

These delicious treats are strong enough to hold shape while also large enough to hold a great amount of filling taking on a variety of flavors. Just perfect for the adventurous consumer.

In this recipe we will be filling ours with beefy tomato rice and shredded cheese, Monterey Jack in this case but feel free to use whatever your heart desires. Protein of choice can also be altered.
